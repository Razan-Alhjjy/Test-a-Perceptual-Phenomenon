(1) What is the independent variable? What is the dependent variable?¶

Independent variable: It is the word and color were the "Congruent" or "Incongruent".
Dependent variable: It is the sum of seconds to select the color of the word.

(2) What is an appropriate set of hypotheses for this task? Specify your null and alternative hypotheses, and clearly define any notation used. Justify your choices.¶

Null Hypothsis:
There is no effect When the word didn't Congruent with color.
H0: i c

Alternate Hypothesis:
Need more second When the word didn't Congruent with color.
H1: i > c


(i = mean of population incongruent ,c = mean of population congruent)

We will choose t-test, cause we have less than 30 samples and dont know the population's standard deviation.
We will choose two tailed , cause we want to know the variances response in the second.
We will choose an independent Paired samples t-tests, cause there are two samples t-test used two separate sets 'Congruent' and 'Incongruent' with the difference in mean involve independent samples.
